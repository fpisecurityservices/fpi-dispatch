How FPI shuts it down
  • RIO solar trailers cover the entire lot
    Mobile, solar-powered surveillance units with AI cameras and thermal night vision. No trenching, no monthly utility bill.

Construction sites

Copper, fuel, tools, equipment.

Cost of inaction

Active sites are wide-open targets. Copper wire, diesel siphoning, skid-steer theft, generator walks — and every loss pushes your schedule and budget. One stolen mini-excavator: $45K + 2 weeks of downtime.

How FPI shuts it down
  • RIO trailers move with the project
    Off-grid solar units relocate phase by phase. No conduit, no contractor, no power source required on site.

  • Court-ready incident reports
